Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How can I find a nearby rental or returning point?

Make sure GPS is enabled while using the “HK Easy Charge App” or “WeChat Mini Programs”. Users can use the “Search” tab to find a specific location or select any nearby location on the map to rent/return a power bank.

Q2. How many power bank can be rented simultaneously?

"Single Rental" user can rent more than one power bank provided the deposit is paid for each power bank. For VIP members, please refer to point 6.

Q3. How to rent a power bank?

Scan the QR code on any charging station using the “SCAN QR CODE” button in the app and click the “Next” button. Deposit must be paid accordingly for non-VIP members. If successful, the power bank will pop up automatically to complete the rental process.

Q4. How much is the rental fee?

The rental fee is HKD$6 per hour with a maximum charge of HKD$30 per day. A deposit fee of HKD$198 should be paid before renting which will be refunded after returning the power bank and paying the rental fee. If the power bank is not returned after 5 days, the deposit fee will not be refunded, and the user can keep the power bank without returning it. For VIP members, please refer to Point 6.

Q5. How to charge your mobile devices by using our power bank in a correct manner?

Users can choose between wired or wireless charging depending on user’s preferences.
1. If wired charging is opted, there are three different types of cables including “Lightning”, “USB Type-C” and “Micro USB”, choose one of the cables that is compatible with your device.
2. If wireless charging is opted, place your mobile device at the back of the power bank (side without the cables).
Make sure the power of the power bank is switched on (look for blue light indicator). Please avoid charging multiple devices simultaneously which may deteriorate the performance of power bank.

Q6. VIP Member Service

The 2-Month VIP Pass is an unlimited rental and deposit free service for frequent users of wireless power bank service. Each rental is free for maximum 48 hours across the city. By clicking the “2-month pass" button, the customer is deemed to have read and accept the terms & conditions.
1. VIP Pass members are required to return the power bank within 48 hours, otherwise the unlimited rental service would be suspended. Late return would cause extra fees based on the normal rental rate (refer to Point 5).
2. Late return of powerbank with more than 5 days will result in termination of the VIP Membership and an additional charge equivalent to 3 days rental fee will be charged.
3. VIP Pass members can only rent one piece of power bank during each rental.
4. Hong Kong Easy Charge Co. Ltd. reserves the right of final decision in case of any dispute arising herein.

Q7. How to return the power bank?

Find any nearby charging station on the map from the app. Insert the wireless charger into an empty slot securely. Lights will be flashing on the charging station indicating the wireless charger is being charged and returned correctly. Check the order under the “Your Order” tab from the directory page ensuring the status of the order is “Returned”.

Q8. How to get my deposit refunded?

After power bank has been returned successfully, go to “Wallet/My Account” from the directory page, then click on “Original deposit refundable” and you will be directed to “Refund of deposit” page. The original payment method will be selected as default. Fill in all the required bank details under “Description” and click “Submit”. Please note that the deposit will be refunded within 7 working days.

Q9. Power bank does not pop up automatically after completing the renting process?

Please try and scan the QR code again. If the problem still prevails, please contact our customer service representative by calling the hotline at (852) 2322-2596.

Q10. Order does not cease after returning the power bank?

Please ensure the power bank is properly returned into an empty charging slot. A “click” sound will be heard when wireless charger is returned securely. If the problem still prevails, please contact our customer service representative by calling the hotline at (852) 2322-2596.

Q11. Encountered any other issues with our apps and services?

Users can choose to report any problems with the service by submitting a feedback form by navigating to the “FAQ” page from the directory page then click “Feedback”.

Q12. Can I rent/return the power bank outside of Hong Kong?

Users can choose to rent/return the power bank in any designated location within the Hong Kong and Macau regions.
